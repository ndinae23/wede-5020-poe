## Project Overview
Miami’s Catering Services is a small business that provides food catering for events such as Parties ,Weddings and corporate functions. The business focuses on delivering high quality meals and excellent customer service .its mission is to provide delicious and affordable food for all occasions .while its vision is to become a trusted catering brand in the community .The target audience includes individuals,families and businesses hosting events


## Website Goals and Objectives
Goal  
The primary goal of this website is to increase bookings for Miami’s Catering by improving our online presence and making it easy for potential customers to find us, view our services, and send an enquiry.

Objectives  
Visibility: Ensure Miami’s Catering appears in Google search results for “catering Pretoria” and related terms within 3 months of launch.  
Lead Generation: Make it simple for visitors to check our menu/services and submit an enquiry form or click WhatsApp in under 3 clicks.  
Credibility: Showcase real photos, testimonials, and service details so 80% of enquiries are from qualified customers ready to book.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)  
We will measure success using:  
Website Traffic – Total number of unique visitors per month.  
Conversion Rate – % of visitors who complete an enquiry form or click “WhatsApp Us.” Target: 5%+ 
Booking Rate – % of enquiries that convert into confirmed, paid bookings. Target: 20%+. 
Engagement – Average pages per session and number of menu/services page views. Target: 2.5+ pages/session.



## Timeline and Milestones

Week 1: Research & Planning  
Goal: Lock down scope so we build the right site, fast.  
Deliverables:  
Competitor Analysis: Review 3 top Pretoria catering sites. Note features, pricing display, and enquiry flows.  
Sitemap: Final page list + structure. Example: Home → About → Services → Enquiry → Contact.  
Content Plan: List of all text, photos, and menus needed from Miami’s Catering.  
Wireframe: Low-fidelity sketch of Home + Services page layout for approval.  
Client action: Sign off wireframe + send raw content by Friday.

Week 2: Design & Layout Development  
Goal: Create the look/feel and get visual approval before coding.  
Deliverables:  
Moodboard: Colour swatches, font samples, button styles in gold/white/dark brown.  
High-Fidelity Mockups: Clickable designs of Home, Services, and Enquiry pages for desktop + mobile.  
Image Treatment: Edit 10–15 client photos for web — crop, color correct, compress.  
Client action: Approve designs OR request 1 round of revisions by Friday.

Week 3: Website Development & Content Integration  
Goal: Build the functional site with all content in place.  
Deliverables:  
Developed Pages: All 5 pages built in WordPress/HTML with responsive layout.  
Functionality: Enquiry form connected to email + auto-reply, WhatsApp button active, menu PDFs uploaded.  
SEO Basics: Page titles, meta descriptions, image alt text added for “catering Pretoria” keywords.  
Staging Link: Private URL sent to client to review the live site.  
Client action: Test on phone/laptop + send change list by Friday.

Week 4: Testing, Debugging & Launch  
Goal: Make it bulletproof, then go live.  
Deliverables:  
Cross-Device Testing: Check site on iPhone, Android, Chrome, Safari. Fix layout bugs.  
Speed + Form Testing: Ensure pages load <3 sec. Test enquiry form delivers to email + triggers auto-reply.  
Final Content Upload: Add testimonials, last-minute menu changes, Halaal certificate.  
Launch: Move site from staging to http://miamiscatering.co.za. Submit to Google.  
Handover: 30-min training + PDF guide on how to update photos/text and view enquiries.  
Client action: Final approval → site goes live
